The Best Movies According To Ankit

My top 3 movies of all-time

3 Idiots

Its a movie which reflects the life of all engineering students. The struggles they had to go through and how do they come out of it.The movie is beautifuuly written and picturized. It is directed by Rajkumar Hirani and stars Aamir Khan, Sharman Joshi, Kareena Kapoor and R Madhavan in the lead.

Bajrangi Bhaijaan

Its another beautifully written masterpiece. The movie is about how the protagonist helps a kid who has been lost in another countrya.

Tumbadd

The movie is shot excellently. Its one of the best horror thriller which has been shot over the years. The combination of mythology and horror will scare everyone and keep them glued to the screen
